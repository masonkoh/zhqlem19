Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) UPDATES: Global

Anyone concerned that they may have been exposed to, or are experiencing symptoms of the COVID-19, should contact their primary care provider, local public health office, or Public Health Agency (1-833-784-4397)

Symtoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of smell
  • Sometimes no symtoms at all

Complications

  • Pneumonia
  • Viral Sepsis
  • Acute respiratory distress syndrome
  • Kidney failure
  • Cytokine release syndrome
